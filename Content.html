PACKS

Expansion Packs

An expansion pack is the most significant content pack available. Expansion packs tend to focus on major new features, and life states are geared towards the pack's major theme. With The Sims 4 Expansion Packs you can take your Sims on new adventures. Experience life in the city, create and join unique Clubs, or get to work with exciting new careers. The following is the list of expansion packs released:

  • Get Famous
  • Seasons
  • Cats and Dogs
  • City Living
  • Get Together
  • Get to Work

Game pack are released very frequently, for now there are 6 game packs available. Here are the list of game packs:

  • Jungle Adventure
  • Parenthood
  • Vampires
  • Dine Out
  • Spa Day
  • Outdoor Retreat

Game Packs

A game pack is a digital-only add-on pack, introduced with The Sims 4: Outdoor Retreat. Game packs are intended to be smaller than full expansion packs but larger than stuff packs. With The Sims 4 Game Packs, you can add new experiences and play in new thematic ways. Have your Sims run a restaurant, go on an outdoor adventure, or enjoy a relaxing day at the spa.

CHEATS

In video games, a cheat is a feature added by the game developers and hidden from normal gameplay that changes the game mechanics. In The Sims series, cheats are typically activated through entering codes into a text window, which can be opened through Ctrl+Shift+C.
